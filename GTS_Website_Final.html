About GTS Enterprises

Trust Once,
Services Best

GTS Enterprises was founded in 2017 with a strong vision of providing reliable and efficient Corrosion Protection (CP) solutions across critical infrastructure sectors.

Our mission is to safeguard pipelines, structures, and facilities using proven anti-corrosion techniques and internationally recognized standards.

Certified cathodic protection engineers and field technicians
Official partner of GAIL for Maharashtra pipeline network
Full compliance with Indian Standards and safety codes
End-to-end project delivery from survey to maintenance
What We Do

Our Services

CP
Cathodic Protection
Design, installation and commissioning of impressed current and sacrificial anode cathodic protection systems for pipelines and structures.
PC & W
Pipeline Coating & Wrapping
Anti-corrosion coating application, holiday testing and pipeline wrapping for new and existing pipeline infrastructure.
SM
CP System Maintenance
Periodic monitoring, testing and maintenance of existing cathodic protection systems to ensure continued effectiveness and compliance.
MS & I
CP Material Supply & Installation
Procurement and installation of anodes, rectifiers, junction boxes and all CP system components.
CIPS / DCVG
Testing & Surveying
Close Interval Potential Survey (CIPS) and Direct Current Voltage Gradient (DCVG) surveys for pipeline integrity assessment.
